In the Euro 2012 final, Spain defeated Italy with a score of 4-0. Spain's goals were scored by David Silva and Jordi Alba. This victory secured Spain's third major international tournament win in a row, following their victories in the Euro 2008 and the 2010 World Cup. Italy's defeat marked the end of their impressive run in the tournament, where they had reached the final despite being considered underdogs.
