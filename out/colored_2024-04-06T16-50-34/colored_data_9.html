The name "Tortuguero" in Costa Rica's Tortuguero National Park translates to "Forest of sea turtles. The park's name reflects the importance of these sea turtles and their nesting activities within the protected area. Visitors to Tortuguero National Park have the opportunity to witness the incredible spectacle of sea turtles coming ashore to lay their eggs, making it a popular ecotourism destination.
